## Content

Lead qualification in our company consists of four main steps:
1. Verify that all mandatory lead data fields are complete.
2. Check whether the company and contact already exist in the system (duplicate check).
3. Assess business fit and confirm ownership of the lead.
4. Decide the outcome: convert to opportunity, reject, or request more information.

Sales Operations is responsible for verifying data completeness.
The sales representative confirms business relevance and ownership.
