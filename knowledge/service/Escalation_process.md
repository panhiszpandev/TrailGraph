# Escalation Process

summary: Rules and triggers for escalating unresolved cases to higher support tiers.
children: []
related: [service/Case_handling.md]

## Content

Cases are escalated automatically when they breach defined SLA response times.
Escalation rules are configured in Salesforce and trigger reassignment to senior agents or managers.
Manual escalation is also possible when the agent determines the case exceeds their competency.
Escalated cases must be resolved within a shorter SLA window.
