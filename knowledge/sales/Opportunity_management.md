# Opportunity Management

summary: Process followed after lead qualification and conversion, covering opportunity stages and closing.
children: []
related: [sales/Lead_qualification.md]

## Content

After a lead is qualified and converted, an opportunity is created in Salesforce.
Opportunity stages: Prospecting, Qualification, Proposal, Negotiation, Closed Won, Closed Lost.
Each stage has defined entry criteria and required fields.
The sales representative owns the opportunity and is responsible for keeping it updated.
